About ADS Framework

The ADS Framework is a deep learning-powered image analysis platform that detects and identifies the source camera of uploaded images. Whether it's for forensic research or security validation, ADS delivers fast, accurate, and transparent analysis — all within a smooth UI.

  • AI-based camera recognition
  • One-click image upload
  • Secure & private processing
  • Fast and responsive interface
